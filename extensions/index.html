Community Initiatives

Name: Research Data Alliance

Sub-categories: Rights, Licensing, Metadata & Ontologies

Started: 2013

Founded by: European Commission, the US National Science Foundation (NSF), and the Australian Government’s Department of Innovation (The Research Data Alliance, 2017).

Current URL: https://www.rd-alliance.org/

Goals: Facilitate data re-use through the development of interest groups and working groups that collaborate to develop outputs such as policies, recommendations, standards, codes, and best practices (Research Data Alliance, 2017c).

Status: Active
